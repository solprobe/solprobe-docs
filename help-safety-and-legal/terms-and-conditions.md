## 1. What SolProbe is

SolProbe is a **pay-per-call Solana token risk scanner** and a **Mode-A BYOW swap executor**:

- **Scanners** (`/scan/quick`, `/market/intel`, `/scan/deep`) read live on-chain and off-chain data and return structured JSON — an A–F structural-safety grade, a BULLISH/BEARISH/NEUTRAL market signal, or an adversarial deep-dive with a BUY/AVOID/WATCH/DYOR recommendation.
- **Swap executor** (`/trade/quote` + `/trade/execute`) returns an unsigned Solana transaction routed through Jupiter for you to sign locally, then broadcasts the signed bytes. **SolProbe never holds your private key.**

The Service is live and actively developed. Features, prices, and response shapes may change; breaking changes will be reflected in `schema_version`.

## 2. Risks and your responsibilities

You acknowledge the risks of using an early-stage, agent-facing, on-chain service:

**a. Software risk.** The Service is actively developed; bugs, scanner blind spots, stale data, and partial outages can happen. We degrade gracefully (`data_quality: "PARTIAL" | "LIMITED"`) where we can, but we don't guarantee uninterrupted service.

**b. On-chain risk.** Calling SolProbe involves real blockchain interaction:
- **Base payments** (`eip155:8453`) are EIP-3009 `transferWithAuthorization` USDC permits to our payment wallet `0x8D2460E139a8BE2fdB72a44a52bc494F7326adfC`. Gas is sponsored by a facilitator.
- **Solana payments** (`solana:5eykt4U…`) are SPL USDC transfers to our payment wallet `FQ3VL8vv8niMMJRY6SVGw6wgJ8bbs5WstN9fK2d6cAc9`. Fees are paid by a facilitator.
- **Swap execution** signs and broadcasts a Solana transaction built by [Jupiter](https://jup.ag). Slippage, MEV, failed routes, partial fills, and price impact are real risks. The risk gate runs in `/trade/quote` and you can reject before signing — once signed and submitted, the transaction is yours.

**c. Scanner limitations.** Structural-safety grading is deterministic and reproducible; the deep-dive includes adversarial signals (clustering, wash trading, dev wallet history) computed from third-party data sources that may be incomplete or stale. A grade of "A" is **not a guarantee that a token is safe** — it means no known structural vector is present at scan time.

**d. AI narrative summaries.** Human-readable summary fields (e.g. `summary`, `full_risk_report`) are generated by a language model. Determined fields (`structural_risk_grade`, `risk_breakdown`, `factors[]`, `recommendation.action`, etc.) are computed in scanner code, not by the LLM. Trust the deterministic fields; verify the narrative.

**e. Test with small amounts.** When connecting a new wallet or a new agent, run one `/scan/quick` ($0.02) first to verify the round trip before you wire up a budget. Do not put more in a wallet than you can afford to lose.

**f. Wallet security.** You are solely responsible for your private keys, seed phrases, and signing infrastructure. We never see them.
